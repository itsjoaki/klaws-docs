title: Your agent's skills
description: How your agent learns and improves.
---

Your agent comes with built-in capabilities and learns new skills over time.

## Built-in capabilities

Your agent can already do all of this — just ask:

- Search the web and summarize anything
- Write code, build websites, create apps
- Create PDFs, Word docs, presentations
- Generate charts and data visualizations
- Preview and deploy sites with Canvas
- Run scheduled tasks while you sleep
- Remember everything across conversations

## Auto-learning

Your agent creates new skills as it works. When it solves something complex, it saves the approach as a reusable skill.

View learned skills in **Dashboard > Skills > Learned**.

## Integrations

Connect external services to give your agent more power:

- **Google Workspace** — Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Sheets (one-click OAuth)
- **X / Twitter** — post, reply, like, retweet
- **Telegram** — chat from your phone
- **Discord** — add to any server

Connect services in **Dashboard > Integrations**.
