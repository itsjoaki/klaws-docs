---
title: Browse the Skills Marketplace
description: Find and install skills for your agent.
---

The Skills Marketplace has **170+ skills** that give your agent new abilities — from sending emails to tracking crypto to generating memes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Skills">
    Go to your [dashboard](https://useklaws.com/dashboard) and click **Skills** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Marketplace">
    Click the **Marketplace** tab to browse available skills.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a skill">
    Browse by category or use the search bar. Each skill shows a description of what it does.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install">
    Click **Install** on any skill. It's available immediately in your next conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Popular categories

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Communication | Email, Phone calls, SMS |
| Creative | Meme generator, Design, Blender 3D |
| Crypto | Solana, Base, wallet tracking |
| Productivity | PDF tools, Notes search, Documents |
| Developer | Docker, Coding assistant, MCP servers |
| Security | 1Password, Supply chain analysis |

## Uninstalling

Go to the **My Skills** tab, find the skill, and click **Remove**.

<Tip>
  Your agent can also learn new skills on its own during conversations. Check the My Skills tab to see what it has picked up.
</Tip>
