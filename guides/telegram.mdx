# Set Up Telegram

Connect your agent to Telegram so you can message it just like any other contact. The whole process takes about 2 minutes.

## What you'll need

- A Telegram account
- The Telegram app (phone or desktop)

## Step-by-step setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open BotFather on Telegram">
    Search for **@BotFather** in Telegram, or [click here to open it](https://t.me/BotFather). BotFather is Telegram's official tool for creating bots.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new bot">
    Send `/newbot` to BotFather. It will ask you two things:

    1. **A name** for your bot (this is the display name, e.g. "My Klaws Agent")
    2. **A username** for your bot (must end in `bot`, e.g. `my_klaws_agent_bot`)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the token">
    BotFather will reply with an **API token** — a long string of numbers and letters. Copy this token. You'll need it in the next step.

    <Warning>
      Keep this token private. Anyone with your token can control your Telegram bot.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the token to Klaws">
    Go to your agent's dashboard, find the **Channels** section, and select Telegram. Paste your bot token and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Go back to Telegram, find your bot by searching for its username, and send it a message. Your agent will respond.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change the bot's name or profile picture?">
    Yes. Go back to BotFather and use the `/setname` or `/setuserpic` commands to customize your bot's appearance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my bot in group chats?">
    Your bot is designed for 1-on-1 conversations. Group chat support may be added in the future.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My bot isn't responding">
    Double-check that you pasted the correct token in your Klaws dashboard. Make sure there are no extra spaces before or after the token.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
