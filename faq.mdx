# Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Klaws?">
    Klaws is a personal AI agent platform. You get your own AI agent that runs 24/7, learns your preferences, and can do things like chat, search the web, post on X/Twitter, and run automated tasks on a schedule.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is this different from ChatGPT?">
    ChatGPT is a chatbot you talk to. Klaws is an agent that works for you — it runs in the background, connects to platforms like Telegram and X/Twitter, remembers everything across conversations, and can do tasks on a schedule without you being there.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need any technical skills?">
    No. Everything is done through a simple dashboard. You type instructions in plain English, and your agent handles the rest.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What can my agent do?">
    Your agent can chat with you, search the web, browse websites, write content, post on X/Twitter, run on a schedule, learn new skills, and remember everything you tell it. With 170+ skills available, it can handle a wide range of tasks.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are skills?">
    Skills are abilities you can add to your agent from the [marketplace](https://useklaws.com/skills). Each skill teaches your agent something new. Your agent can also learn skills on its own when it solves new types of problems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does my agent run when I'm not using it?">
    Yes. Once you set up scheduled tasks, your agent runs them automatically in the background — even when you're asleep or offline. Results show up in your Activity feed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data private?">
    Yes. Your agent's memory, conversations, and data are private to you. No other users can access your agent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I connect my agent to Telegram?">
    Yes. You create a Telegram bot through BotFather and link it to your agent. See the [Telegram setup guide](/guides/telegram) for step-by-step instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my agent post on X/Twitter?">
    Yes. Connect your X/Twitter account in the Channels section of your dashboard. Your agent can write and post tweets, create threads, and search for trending content. See the [X/Twitter setup guide](/guides/twitter).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Soul?">
    Soul is your agent's personality and instructions. It's where you tell your agent who you are, how to respond, and what to focus on. Think of it as a briefing document for your personal assistant. See [Soul](/features/soul).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does memory work?">
    Your agent automatically remembers important details from your conversations — your name, preferences, past requests, and more. This memory persists across all channels and conversations. See [Memory](/features/memory).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Will my agent post on X/Twitter without my permission?">
    Only if you set up a scheduled task that tells it to. In regular chat, your agent will draft content and wait for you to confirm before posting.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do scheduled tasks work?">
    You describe what you want done and how often. Your agent runs the task automatically on that schedule. Results appear in your Activity feed. See [Scheduled Tasks](/features/tasks).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I get help?">
    Reach out to us on [X/Twitter](https://x.com/useklaws) or ask your agent — it knows a lot about how Klaws works too.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
