title: Settings
description: Manage your account and agent.
---

Access Settings from the user menu at the bottom of the sidebar.

## Profile

View your email and username.

## Subscription

See your current plan, trial status, and manage your subscription.

- [View pricing](https://useklaws.com/pricing) to upgrade or change plans
- Manage billing through the Stripe customer portal

## Agent info

View your agent's name, status, number of skills, and scheduled tasks.

## Intelligence

Your agent automatically selects the best AI model for each task — fast models for simple questions, high-quality models for writing and analysis. It also auto-detects your timezone so scheduled tasks run at the right local time.

## Danger zone

<Warning>
  These actions are irreversible.
</Warning>

- **Reset agent** — clears all memory, skills, and conversation history. Your agent keeps running but starts completely fresh.
- **Delete agent** — permanently removes your agent and all its data.
