Skills are capabilities you can add to your agent. The Skills tab has two sections:

## My Skills

Shows all skills your agent currently has — both manually installed and auto-learned.

<Info>
  Your agent can **learn new skills on its own** during conversations. When you ask it to do something new, it may create a skill to remember how to do it next time.
</Info>

You can remove any skill by clicking **Remove** next to it.
