The **Soul** is your agent's personality and instructions. It controls the tone, style, and behavior of every response.

## Presets

Choose a preset to get started quickly:

| Preset | Style |
|--------|-------|
| **Default** | Balanced, helpful, proactive |
| **Creative** | Imaginative, uses metaphors, bold ideas |
| **Concise** | 1-2 sentences max, no fluff |
| **Professional** | Formal, structured, business-like |
| **Friendly** | Warm, casual, encouraging |

## Custom personality

Want something specific? Click **Edit** and write your own instructions in plain text.

**Examples:**
- "You are a fitness coach. Be motivating and give practical workout advice."
- "You are my personal news curator. Summarize everything in bullet points. Focus on tech and crypto."
- "Always respond in Spanish. Be casual and use humor."

<Note>
  Changes take effect on your next message. No need to redeploy.
</Note>
