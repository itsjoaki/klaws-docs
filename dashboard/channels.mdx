The Integrations page lets you connect your agent to external services — one click for most.

## Messaging

### Telegram

Connect your agent to Telegram and chat from your phone. Your agent responds 24/7.

<Card title="Connect Telegram" icon="paper-plane" href="/guides/telegram">
  Step-by-step setup guide
</Card>

### Discord

Add your agent to any Discord server. Paste your bot token from the [Discord Developer Portal](https://discord.com/developers/applications).

### Coming soon

- **WhatsApp** — in development

## Apps

### Google Workspace

Connect Gmail, Google Calendar, Drive, and Sheets with one click. Click **Connect** and authorize with your Google account — your agent gets full access.

### X / Twitter

Post tweets, reply, like, and retweet. Click **Connect** and paste your API credentials from the [X Developer Portal](https://developer.x.com).

### Coming soon

- **Notion** — notes, wikis, databases
- **Slack** — team messaging
- **GitHub** — repos, issues, PRs

<Info>
  Your agent shares the same memory and skills across all channels and integrations. A conversation on Telegram informs responses on the web, and vice versa.
</Info>
