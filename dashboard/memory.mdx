Your agent has persistent memory — it remembers facts, preferences, and context from every conversation, across all channels.

## How it works

- Your agent automatically saves important information during conversations
- Memories persist between sessions and across web chat and Telegram
- You can search memories by keyword or browse all of them

## Viewing memories

Go to the **Memory** tab and click **Load Memories** to see everything your agent has saved. Use the search bar to find specific topics.

<Warning>
  **Resetting your agent** (in Settings) erases all memories permanently. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>
