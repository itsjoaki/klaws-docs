## Managing your subscription

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime from **Settings > Subscription** in your dashboard. This opens the Stripe billing portal where you can:

- Change your plan
- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Cancel subscription

<Info>
  When your trial ends, choose a plan to keep using your agent. Your data is safe — nothing gets deleted.
</Info>
