# Skills

Skills give your agent new abilities. Think of them like apps for your phone — each one lets your agent do something new.

## The Skills Marketplace

Browse 170+ skills in the [marketplace](https://useklaws.com/skills). Skills cover a wide range of tasks:

- **Social media** — Post on X/Twitter, write threads, manage content
- **Research** — Search the web, analyze websites, summarize articles
- **Writing** — Draft emails, blog posts, newsletters, reports
- **Productivity** — Manage schedules, take notes, organize information
- And much more

## Installing a skill

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the marketplace">
    Go to the [Skills page](https://useklaws.com/skills) and find a skill you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Install">
    Hit the install button. That's it — your agent can now use the skill.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using it">
    Just ask your agent to do something related to the skill. It knows what it has available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auto-learning

Here's the cool part: **your agent can learn new skills on its own.**

When you ask your agent to do something complex that it doesn't already know how to do, it will figure out the best approach and save that as a new skill for next time. You don't need to do anything — it just gets smarter over time.

<Tip>
  Check your agent's installed skills from time to time. You might be surprised by what it has picked up on its own.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Auto-learned skills are private to your agent. Skills from the marketplace are shared across the platform.
</Note>
