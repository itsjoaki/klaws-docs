# Activity Feed

The Activity feed is your agent's logbook. It shows you everything your agent has done, so you always know what's going on.

## What shows up in the feed

- **Scheduled task results** — Every time a [scheduled task](/features/tasks) runs, the result appears here. You can see exactly what your agent did and what it found.
- **Telegram conversations** — Messages exchanged with your agent on [Telegram](/guides/telegram) show up in the feed.
- **Important events** — Skill installs, errors, and other noteworthy events.

## Why it matters

Your agent runs 24/7, even when you're not looking. The Activity feed lets you:

- **Check results** — See what your scheduled tasks produced overnight
- **Spot issues** — Notice if a task failed or returned unexpected results
- **Stay in the loop** — Know exactly what your agent has been up to

<Tip>
  Check your Activity feed regularly, especially after setting up new scheduled tasks. It's the easiest way to make sure everything is working as expected.
</Tip>
