# Channels

Channels are the different ways you can interact with your agent. Right now, your agent is available on:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Web Chat" icon="globe" href="/features/chat">
    Built-in chat on your dashboard. Always available, no setup needed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Telegram" icon="paper-plane" href="/guides/telegram">
    Message your agent from Telegram like you would any contact.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Connecting a channel

Each channel has its own setup process. The web chat works out of the box. For Telegram, you'll need to create a bot through Telegram's BotFather and link it to your agent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a channel">
    Go to your agent's dashboard and find the Channels section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the setup guide">
    Each channel has a step-by-step guide. See [Telegram setup](/guides/telegram) or [X/Twitter setup](/guides/twitter).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    Once connected, your agent responds on that channel just like it does on the web.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## All channels share the same brain

Your agent's personality ([Soul](/features/soul)), [memory](/features/memory), and [skills](/features/skills) work across every channel. A conversation on Telegram is just as good as one on the web.
