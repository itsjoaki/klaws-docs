# Soul

Soul is your agent's personality. It controls how your agent talks, what it knows about you, and how it approaches tasks.

## What goes in a Soul?

Think of it as instructions you'd give a new assistant on their first day:

- **Who you are** — your name, what you do, your goals
- **How to respond** — casual or formal, short or detailed, funny or serious
- **What to focus on** — topics you care about, your industry, your audience
- **What to avoid** — things you don't want your agent to say or do

## How to edit your Soul

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Go to your agent's dashboard and find the **Soul** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your instructions">
    Type plain-text instructions describing your agent's personality and behavior. No special format needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Hit save. Your agent immediately starts using the updated Soul in all conversations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example

Here's a simple Soul:

```
You are a helpful assistant for Sarah, a freelance designer.
Keep responses short and casual. Use bullet points when possible.
Sarah's clients are small businesses and startups.
When writing social media posts, use a friendly and approachable tone.
Never use corporate buzzwords.
```

<Tip>
  Start simple and add more instructions as you go. Your agent also learns from conversations, so you don't need to put everything in the Soul upfront.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Changes to your Soul apply to all channels — web chat, Telegram, scheduled tasks, and everything else.
</Note>
