# Chat

Your agent is always available for a conversation. Open the web chat, type your message, and get a response in seconds.

## How it works

Your agent reads your message, thinks about it, and replies. It can also:

- **Search the web** to find up-to-date information
- **Browse websites** to read articles, docs, or pages you share
- **Use skills** to handle specific tasks like writing posts or analyzing data
- **Remember context** from previous conversations

Every conversation is saved in your agent's memory, so it gets better the more you talk to it.

## Tips for better results

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Be specific" icon="bullseye">
    Instead of "write something about AI," try "write a 3-sentence LinkedIn post about AI agents for small business owners."
  </Card>
  <Card title="Give context" icon="circle-info">
    Tell your agent who you are, what you do, and what tone you prefer. It remembers everything.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ask follow-ups" icon="comments">
    Your agent remembers the current conversation. Ask it to revise, expand, or change anything.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share links" icon="link">
    Paste a URL and your agent will read the page and work with the content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
