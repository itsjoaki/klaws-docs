---
title: Create your agent
description: Go from zero to chatting with your AI agent in under 2 minutes.
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [useklaws.com](https://useklaws.com) and sign in with Google or a magic link email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your agent">
    Pick a name for your agent — this is how it introduces itself. You can change it later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a personality">
    Choose a preset personality or write your own:
    - **Default** — Friendly, helpful, and proactive
    - **Creative** — Imaginative, bold ideas, vivid language
    - **Concise** — Short answers, no fluff
    - **Professional** — Formal, structured, thorough
    - **Friendly** — Warm, casual, encouraging
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Hit **Deploy**. Your agent will be ready in about 60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start chatting">
    You'll land on the Chat screen. Say hello — your agent is live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Want to talk to your agent on your phone? See the [Telegram guide](/guides/telegram).
</Tip>
